Why Clover?

The Clover story begins more than 40 years ago with a mother's love

of all music. I grew up enveloped by music in one form or another from my

pocket radio to mother's huge record collection. Unable to carry a tune but with

an exceptional ear, led to a career at the local radio station.

Which I managed through grace and luck to develope into a national

position and that is what brought about the clover part of this story.

My love had become a chore, to such a great degree I considered a

change. My contemplation guided me to a new love, my future wife. She

was laying in the middle of a patch of clover in search of a four-leafed one.

My literally stumbing across her led to romance, love, marriage, and a never

before examined path back to my first passion in the form of this record store.

If you made it this far, come in for a visit and maybe I have something

new or not so new for your listening pleasure.
